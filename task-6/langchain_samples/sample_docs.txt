COMPANY HANDBOOK

Vacation Policy:
All full-time employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year. Vacation days accrue monthly at a rate of 1.25 days per month. Employees must request vacation at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal.

Remote Work Policy:
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work requests must be approved by direct managers. All remote workers must be available during core hours (10am-3pm EST).

Health Benefits:
The company provides comprehensive health insurance including medical, dental, and vision coverage. Employees can enroll during the annual open enrollment period or within 30 days of hire. The company covers 80% of premium costs.

Professional Development:
Employees have access to an annual professional development budget of $2,000. This can be used for courses, conferences, certifications, or books related to your role.
